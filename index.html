The Prompt Framework

Use this every time you prompt.

01 ——
Task
What exactly do you want done? Be specific — explain, summarize, critique, draft.
02 ——
Context
Who is this for? What's the background? The machine can't read your mind.
03 ——
Format
What should the output look like? List, paragraph, table, slide bullets?
04 ——
Tone
What's the required voice and reading level? Professional, accessible, academic?

The machine didn't get smarter — your guidance became clearer.
